Verify My Email

http://localhost:3000/api/users/verify-email?token=test_verification_token

The Learn2Grow Team

Note: If you're not seeing the verification button in your actual emails, there might be an issue with your email client or how it's rendering HTML emails.

Troubleshooting:

  1. Check if HTML emails are enabled in your email client
  2. Try viewing the email in a different email client
  3. Check if your email client is blocking images or external content
  4. Verify that the template ID in your email service matches the content you want to send
